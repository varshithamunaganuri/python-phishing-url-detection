Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I register for an account?

To register, enter your name, email, mobile number, password, and confirm your password. Click the Register button to complete the process.

2. What should I do if I already have an account?

Click the Login button and enter your registered email and password to access your account.

4. How do I check if a URL is phishing or safe?

Paste the URL into the provided input field and click the Check button. The system will analyze and display a security score.

5. What does the security score indicate?

The score represents the likelihood of the URL being a phishing site. A higher score means the site is safer, while a lower score indicates potential risks.

6. What happens if the URL I entered is a phishing site?

If the site is detected as phishing, avoid entering personal information and close the page immediately.

7. Can I check multiple URLs?

Yes, you can check as many URLs as needed by entering them one at a time.

8. Is my browsing history saved?

No, we do not store any URLs you check or your browsing activity.

9. Do I need an account to check a URL?

Yes, you must be logged in to use the phishing detection tool.

10. How does the phishing detection system work?

It uses machine learning algorithms to analyze the URL structure, domain reputation, and content patterns to determine the likelihood of phishing.

11. Is this service free to use?

Yes, our phishing detection service is free for all registered users.

12. Is my personal data safe with this website?

Yes, we ensure your personal data is secure and not shared with any third parties.

13. Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, the website is mobile-friendly and works on all devices.
